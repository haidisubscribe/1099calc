Last updated: June 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how FreelanceCalc ("we," "our," "the site") handles information when you visit 1099calc.tools. We built this site with a simple principle: collect as little data as possible.

Calculator data

All calculations on this site run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Numbers you enter into any calculator — income, expenses, rates, or any other figure — are never transmitted to a server, stored in a database, or logged anywhere. When you close or refresh the page, that data is gone.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or by visiting www.aboutads.info to opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising.

Analytics

We may use standard web analytics tools to understand aggregate traffic patterns, such as which pages are visited and how many people use the site. This data is anonymized and not linked to any personal identity.

Third-party links

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.
